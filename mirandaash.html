In this interactive workshop you can experience and understand the freedom-centered principles that have built and transformed some of the most democratic workplaces in the world. We begin with a brief introduction to how the principles and design of organisational democracy feed into decision making at all levels of the organisation.

Weâ€™ll examine key principles, including Dialogue + Listening, the balance of Individual + Collective, Fairness + Dignity, Choice and Reflection and Evaluation. These all play a role in helping teams to make good decisions.

Working in small groups, you can start prototyping your own decision-making model with advice from Miranda on how to make the principles work for your specific context.

    By the end of the session you will have:
  • a thorough understanding of democratic decision making, its benefits and challenges
  • an appreciation of the principles required to enable it
  • an initial model or prototype of how democratic decision-making might work in your context
  • a next steps plan for sharing and working on your model with your orgaisation

About Miranda Ash

Miranda leads the global WorldBlu community and The WorldBlu List of Most Democratic Workplaces. The only global award of it's kind that recognises workplaces striving to unleash the potential of every employee through freedom centered and democratic design. She regularly coaches individuals and speaks about freedom centered businesses and workplace democracy at events across the world.
