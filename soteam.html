Changing Cynics: turning blockers into backers.

Book now!
10 places remaining

What to do with the cynic and the sceptic? You know, the one who closes down ideas, who blocks change, who stands like a black cloud on every horizon muttering ’it’ll never work’?

It’s tempting to try and ignore or bypass such people – or get trapped into a cycle of confrontation. Instead this workshop focuses on how all of us exhibit these characteristics at different times and how we can turn what seems like a negative behaviour into a positive force that takes your idea, your team and your organisation forwards.

Highly practical and focused, this workshop asks you to bring problems and concerns from your own organisation that you can work on in an intensive 2 hour session. By the end you’ll be astonished by what you’ve learnt about your own behaviour and that of others. With a valuable set of techniques and tools, you’ll be equipped to take on the cynicism and blocking behaviours around you to turn the negative into a positive force for your organisation.

  • What lies behind cynicism? Understand our programmed responses.
  • Beyond no. Ways to engage with cynical voices
  • The best backers. The tools and skills that help you turn cynics into advocates
