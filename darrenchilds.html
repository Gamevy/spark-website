About Darren Childs & Jane Sparrow

With a prestigious career in broadcast media spanning the globe and two decades, Darren has delivered growth across large media portfolios around the world.

Darren joined UKTV, themulti-award winning media companywhich reaches 42 million viewers per month, in September 2010 with the primary objective of driving the profitability and development of the multiplatform network. Darren’s achievements for the network include positioning Dave as the UK’s most watched non-PSB channel; delivering the most successful year in UKTV’s 21 year history and achieving terrestrial levels of viewership in terms of SOCI. UKTV is proud to be the only British broadcaster recognised by Best Companies.

Before starting at UKTV, Darren was Managing Director of BBC Worldwide Global Channels, where he was responsible for growing and managing the BBC’s extensive channels business, both domestically and internationally, as well as advertising sales across this portfolio and digital media. Under Darren’s leadership, BBC Worldwide Channels achieved dramatic expansion and enjoyed record sales and profits. During this time, he was a member of BBC Worldwide’s Board and Executive Committee Team, and also sat on the UKTV Board.

Prior to BBC Worldwide, Darren was Senior Vice President, International Networks for Sony Pictures Television International (SPTI), EMEA. Before that, he spent several years in Asia, first as Director of Programming for MTV Asia and then as Director of Business Development at Channel V (part of the STAR TV platform).

He has also sat on the non-executive board for HBO Europe.

Jane Sparrow is passionate about enabling others to sustainably perform at their best and achieve their personal and organisational goals. She is an expert facilitator, business consultant, performance coach and impactful speaker. Jane is author of The Culture Builders; Leadership Strategies for Employee Performance and her book is used in leadership and management programmes across the world. It’s been endorsed by business leaders from organisations such as BUPA, Diageo, Sony, Sky, BBC WorldWide and Starbucks.

Her research, experience and development of a bespoke profiling tool to enhance the capability of managers to engage others for performance is widely acknowledged across business and academic communities. Her work has consequently been incorporated into multiple university and business school curriculums, including MBA programmes.

Her opinion and expertise is published regularly across the HR, Comms and business media including the Huffington Post, HR Magazine, HR Director, Strategic HR Review, HR Review Middle East, Strategic Communicator, Communication World and Training Journal.

Jane works closely with people of all levels to help them maximise their potential, build capacity and implement behavioural change programmes within different cultures and a changing business environment. People she has worked with include Sony, UKTV, Sky, Hatchette, Google, BBC, CHP, The British Army, HP, Novartis, Save the Children, Shopzilla, and WPP. This experience has given her strong views on what does and doesn’t lead to peak performance in the business world.
