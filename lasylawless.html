About Lasy Lawless

We're a consultancy specialising in helping businesses develop 21st century culture and leadership - for a digital and more purposeful world.

Our founders are veterans of organisational development, lean business, and the digital world: Pete Burden, Lasy Lawless and Jamie Pyper.

Our difference: we’re all ‘hybrids’ combining in-depth, hands-on business experience with the psychological and OD skills needed to create enduring change in real businesses.
