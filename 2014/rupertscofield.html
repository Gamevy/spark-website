Rupert Scofield tells the story of how he and his colleagues went from his partner's spare bedroom on Broadway and 67th street to a global microfinance network operating in 23 countries on 4 continents, turning the Global Financial System on its head.

About Rupert Scofield

Rupert Scofield is a social entrepreneur with 40 years of experience working in developing countries. He is the CEO of FINCA, a global microfinance organisation operating in 23 countries across 4 continents with over one million clients.

The son of a New York banker, Scofield resolved at an early age that he would never work for "the man" and would never, ever, have a career in banking.

His accident-prone career has meant a lifetime of dodging revolutions, earthquakes, and assassins in the Third World, and once running for his life from a mob in Mogadishu, Somalia.

He is passionate about inspiring the next generation of leaders who want to solve big problems like world poverty.
